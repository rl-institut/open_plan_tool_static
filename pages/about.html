About open_plan

open_plan is a Python-based, open-source energy system modeling tool targeted at simulating energy cells e.g. energy systems for industry parks and districts, or mini-grids.

It uses the oemof framework to describe and optimize the energy system and will provide a user-friendly web-based interface to intereact with python. This will enable users without python or programming knowledge to use the tool and simulate energy systems.
